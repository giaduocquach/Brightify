Planet, Sun and Moon textures in this folder are from
Solar System Scope (https://www.solarsystemscope.com/textures/),
licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

The textures are based on NASA elevation and imagery data; colours and shades
are tuned to true-colour photos from Messenger, Viking, Cassini and the
Hubble Space Telescope.

Attribution: "Textures © Solar System Scope (CC BY 4.0)".

2K base maps (bundled): 2k_sun, 2k_mercury, 2k_venus_atmosphere, 2k_earth_daymap,
2k_earth_clouds, 2k_earth_nightmap, 2k_moon, 2k_mars, 2k_jupiter, 2k_saturn,
2k_saturn_ring_alpha, 2k_uranus, 2k_neptune, 2k_pluto, 2k_ceres, 2k_eris.

Dwarf planets (Solar System Scope, CC BY 4.0): 2k_ceres is based on real Dawn
mission imagery; 2k_eris is an artist's impression (Eris has no surface imagery —
no spacecraft has visited it).

High-res 4K hero maps (in public/textures4k/, loaded on demand for the focused
planet on capable devices; downscaled from Solar System Scope 8K originals):
4k_mercury, 4k_earth_daymap, 4k_mars, 4k_jupiter, 4k_saturn.

Milky Way backdrop: 4k_milkyway_panorama.jpg — from the ESO GigaGalaxy Zoom
"Milky Way panorama" (eso0932a), © ESO/S. Brunier, licensed CC BY 4.0
(https://www.eso.org/public/images/eso0932a/). 360° equirectangular all-sky.
