Planet, Sun, Moon and Milky-Way textures in this folder are from
Solar System Scope (https://www.solarsystemscope.com/textures/),
licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

The textures are based on NASA elevation and imagery data; colours and shades
are tuned to true-colour photos from Messenger, Viking, Cassini and the
Hubble Space Telescope.

Attribution: "Textures © Solar System Scope (CC BY 4.0)".

Files: 2k_sun, 2k_mercury, 2k_venus_atmosphere, 2k_earth_daymap,
2k_earth_clouds, 2k_moon, 2k_mars, 2k_jupiter, 2k_saturn,
2k_saturn_ring_alpha, 2k_uranus, 2k_neptune, 2k_stars_milky_way.

Pluto, the comet and the black hole are rendered procedurally (no texture).
